				         Maggi Recipe

Ingredients:
1] Maggi Noodles Packet
2] Cheese
3] Chilly Flakes

Instructions:
Add water in pan and let it come to boil. Then add in Maggi Masala sachet and stir it well and wait for few minutes. Add in the noodles and let it cook for 10 mins.
Then once cooked, turn off the flame and add grated cheese and chilly flakes. Serve hot!!!
